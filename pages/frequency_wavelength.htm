Calculate missing component between frequency, wavelength, and velocity given two inputs. Velocity factor can be used, or solved for by inputting a velocity (close to the speed of light) then pressing the [V->VF] button.

[*] Metric SI unit prefixes such as p=pico, n=nano, u=micro, m=milli, k=kilo, M=mega are valid.
