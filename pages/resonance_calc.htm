LC Resonance Calculator

⬗ Further Information

Given capacitance and inductance, calculates the resonant frequency of an RLC network. Given either inductance or capacitance and frequency, calculates the missing value. Providing resistance will give the Q factor and maximum dynamic impedance of the network.

[*] Metric SI unit prefixes such as p=pico, n=nano, u=micro, m=milli, k=kilo, M=mega are valid.
