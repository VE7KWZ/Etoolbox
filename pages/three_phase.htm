Three Phase Calculator

⬗ Further Information

Converts between Line and Phase currents/voltages and calculates apparent, real, and reactive power, with power factor and phase angle for a balanced three phase circuit.

[*] Metric SI unit prefixes such as p=pico, n=nano, u=micro, m=milli, k=kilo, M=mega are valid.
