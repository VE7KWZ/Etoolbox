Three Phase Calculator

⮞ Further Information

Converts between Line and Phase currents/voltages and calculates apparent, real, and reactive power, with power factor and phase angle.

Metric (SI) suffixes such as p=pico, n=nano, u=micro, m=milli, k=kilo, M=mega, G=giga are valid.
