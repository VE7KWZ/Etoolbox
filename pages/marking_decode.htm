SMD Resistor/Capacitor Marking Decoder

⬗ Further Information

Decode the 3 or 4 digit markings on SMD resistors, SMD capacitors and some ceramic capacitors.
This tool identifies and decodes 3 and 4 digit EIA codes for resistors and capacitors as well as EIA-96 codes for resistors and EIA-198 codes for capacitors.
